Flex rules

  • Minimum weekly target: 1 lower-body strength session + 1 power/prehab session.
  • Do not stack hard tennis + hard beach + Strength B on back-to-back nights if soreness is rising.
  • If court/class slots fall through, use the condensed workout or prehab.

Workouts & References

Strength A — Jump foundation

  1. Squat pattern — 4 × 5–6
  2. Romanian deadlift — 3 × 8
  3. Bulgarian split squat — 3 × 8 each
  4. Hip thrust / glute bridge — 3 × 10
  5. Standing calf raise — 3 × 12–15
  6. Tibialis raise — 3 × 15–20

Strength B — Power + prehab

  1. Approach jumps — 4 × 3
  2. Lateral bounds — 3 × 5 each
  3. Push-ups — 3 × 8–12
  4. Pull-ups / band rows — 3 × 6–10
  5. Single-leg RDL — 3 × 8 each
  6. Band external rotation + face pull

Sand defender speed block

  1. Split-step reaction — 4 × 45 sec
  2. Short-ball pickup — 4 × 6
  3. Deep line / angle chase — 4 × 5
  4. Controlled sprawl progression — 3 × 5 each

Condensed 30-minute option

  1. Approach jumps — 4 × 3
  2. Bulgarian split squat — 3 × 8 each
  3. RDL or single-leg RDL — 3 × 8
  4. Calf raise + tibialis raise
